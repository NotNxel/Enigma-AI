About Enigma

The Unknown Awaits.

Enigma is a tiny AI lab built by two 10th‑grade students who love turning late‑night “vibe coded” ideas into real, usable tools for developers and creatives.

Who we are

Neel Nikhil Pappu is the co‑founder and CEO of Enigma, specializing in Artificial Intelligence, Fullstack Development and Data Science, He is focusing on product vision, experience, and how AI can feel less like a black box and more like a creative teammate.

Reyansh Verma is the co‑founder and core developer at Enigma, specializing in JavaScript and C++, and building the systems that make our ideas actually run.

What Enigma does

Enigma AI is an experimental tool that helps people “vibecode”: you describe what you want in plain language, and Enigma helps you move from fuzzy idea to structured code or a working prototype faster.

The goal is not to replace developers, but to give students, indie hackers, and small teams a smarter way to explore concepts, test flows, and ship more confidently.
